About KnowledgePlant

Your trusted partner in bringing nature closer to your home

Our Story

KnowledgePlant was founded with a simple mission: to make the beauty and benefits of plants accessible to everyone. We believe that plants not only enhance the aesthetic appeal of our spaces but also contribute significantly to our physical and mental well-being.

Started as a small nursery in Bangladesh, we've grown into a comprehensive online platform offering a wide range of plants, gardening books, and professional decoration services. Our team consists of passionate plant enthusiasts and gardening experts dedicated to helping you create greener, healthier living spaces.
